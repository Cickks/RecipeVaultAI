# Product Requirements Document

## Product
RecipeVault AI is a premium personal cookbook for people who save recipes from social media, websites, family, friends, screenshots, and notes.

## Mission
Create the easiest and most beautiful recipe application available. Users should never lose a recipe again.

## Platforms
- iOS
- Android
- One Expo/React Native codebase

## Target Users
- Home cooks who collect recipes from social media.
- Meal planners who want organized weekly cooking.
- Families preserving personal recipes.
- Fitness and wellness users tracking nutrition.

## Core Jobs
- Save recipes manually.
- Organize recipes into collections and tags.
- Search by title, ingredient, cuisine, nutrition, source, favorites, and natural language.
- Plan meals and generate grocery lists.
- Import pasted recipe text into structured recipes with AI without violating platform terms.

## MVP Scope
- Supabase Auth foundation.
- Recipe dashboard.
- Recipe list, detail, create/edit foundation.
- Collections and favorites.
- Grocery list and meal planner screens.
- Profile/settings.
- Supabase schema with RLS.
- Sample data and production-ready architecture.

## Future Scope
- AI import agents.
- OCR and nutrition estimation.
- RevenueCat Pro and subscription entitlements.
- Offline sync conflict resolution.
- Admin dashboard.
- App Store and Google Play assets.

## Success Metrics
- Recipe saves per active user.
- Search usage and success.
- Meal plans created.
- Grocery list generation.
- AI import conversion.
- Premium conversion.
- Day 7 and day 30 retention.
